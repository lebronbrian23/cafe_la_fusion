about us

La Fusion is a wonderful blend of vibrant, art-inspired events and all-day long dining experiences in a welcoming, French-inspired atmosphere. A one-of-a-kind Gallery-grill with retro pop music and social interactions. Art enthusiasts of all ages and journeys of life are invited to an exciting community of like-minded, creative people and local artists, for Cultural Talks, Jam Sessions, Concerts, Workshops, Movie nights and much more.

our story

La Fusion is committed to honor and embrace the minds and works of young Toronto-based artists whose work includes poetry compositions, fiction and non-fiction literature, vocal and Instrumental performances, spoken word and much more. We intend to be Toronto’s favorite place for a cultural hub.

our chef

marc pierrot executive chef

Chef Marc Pierrot starred in Montreal-based restaurants, leading them to the most popular spots in the province of Quebec. His partner Yves Dobois is a Toronto-based artist consultant and culinary enthusiast. Back in 2017, their passion for Arts & Culinary merged with their entrepreneurial spirits to take their dream out of paper: a place where enthusiasts of Arts can find and enjoy legit Canadian cuisine and creative performances in one place, along with social interactions.
