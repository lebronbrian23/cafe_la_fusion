be a featured artist

Your voice and talent matter to Us

La Fusion has a great collection of local artists in the programming all year long, in addition to special events sponsored by corporate partners. Musicians, game designers, hobbyist painters, concept artists, art teachers and professional illustrators, you belong here and are very welcome.

Let’s talk? Send us a portfolio

If you’re trying to find an opportunity to perform and showcase your artwork, along with your website and social channels, Contact us to share your portfolio and book an appointment.

Our weekly schedule includes talks on painting styles, drawing tips, musical performances, poetry, media & Design, and a lot more. The opportunities are endless to showcase your talent and creativity.

be a business partner

Align your brand with La Fusion

We recognize that the arts play an important role in supporting vibrant communities and strong economies, while fostering meaningful business objectives, such as branding, PR, employee engagement, client hosting.

Consider accessing Canadian artists while telling your brand story in a variety of ways, engaging the next generation: millennials. Themed events, cocktails and artful activations to engage your audience are viable, through maps, projections, installations and more. All in addition to branded napkins, barware, and bottles.
